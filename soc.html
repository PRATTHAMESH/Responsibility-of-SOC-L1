DEEP DIVE: SOC L1 ANALYST

SOC Analysts are the first line of defense against cyber threats, monitoring and analyzing security events to protect organizational assets.

SECURITY EVENT CORRELATION

Identifying patterns across multiple security events to detect potential threats and attacks.

LOG ANALYSIS

Examining system and network logs to identify anomalies and potential security incidents.

THREAT HUNTING (BASIC)

Proactively searching for indicators of compromise and potential threats in the environment.

ESCALATION & COLLABORATION

Working with other teams to resolve security incidents and escalate when necessary.

INITIAL FORENSIC TRIAGE

Performing initial investigation and evidence gathering for potential security incidents.

INCIDENT PRIORITY

Assessing and prioritizing security incidents based on severity and potential impact.

PLAYBOOK EXECUTION

Following established procedures and playbooks for incident response and mitigation.

CONTINUOUS LEARNING

Staying updated with the latest threats, vulnerabilities, and security technologies.
